## The Event — Elsewhere (formerly Nowhere)

### Basics

- **Event name:** Elsewhere
- **Former name:** Nowhere
- **Dates (2026):** July 7–12, 2026
- **Location:** Monegros Desert, Spain
- **Nature:** Community-built temporary city — *not* a traditional festival
- **Organizer:** Nobodies Collective Association (Spanish non-profit)

### Philosophy

Elsewhere is a social experiment emphasizing collective creation rather than consumption. Participants build and then completely dismantle a temporary desert city, leaving zero environmental trace. The tagline: *"building something wild, together, every summer."*

### The Lingo

- **Nobodies** — participants / burners (everyone who attends)
- **Playa** — the event site / desert floor (borrowed from Burning Man)
- **Barrio** — self-organized camping community within the event (Spanish for "neighbourhood")
- **Norg** — Nowhere Organization (legacy term still used by long-timers)
- **Humans platform** — the community platform at humans.nobodies.team for profiles, barrios, teams

### Ten Principles

1. **Consent** — Give only what's appreciated. Take only what's offered.
2. **Community** — Mutual support among participants
3. **Cooperation** — Collaborative problem-solving and construction
4. **Gifting** — Freely given time, effort, and goods without expectation of return
5. **Immediacy** — Full presence in the moment
6. **Inclusion** — Welcoming all participants as equals
7. **Leave No Trace** — Environmental stewardship and complete cleanup
8. **No Commerce** — Zero cash transactions; gifting-based economy
9. **Participation** — Active co-creation required (no spectators)
10. **Self Expression & Self Reliance** — Individual autonomy with personal responsibility

## Wonderever Barrio

### Identity

- **Name:** Wonderever
- **Type:** Barrio (self-organized camp) within Elsewhere/Nowhere
- **Active since:** 2013 (10+ years)
- **Member name:** Wonderlings (members of Wonderever are called "Wonderlings")
- **Tagline / self-description:** *"the best kept secret on the playa"*
- **Self-aware humor:** explicitly describes itself as "totally not a cult"

### The Pitch (verbatim from sources)

> "Wonderever is the best kept secret on the playa. Since 2013 we've harboured a web of wonderful wanderers that work hard, play hard and take care of each other in the heat!"

> "We aim at being a chill barrio, of around 35 and more co-creators."

> "Our organization will be no-stress and relax-y, yet efficient."

> "We will provide Nowhere with a chill living-room, to gather and wonder."

### Size & Community

- Target size: ~35–40 co-creators
- Current (2026 profile): 40 members
- Languages: English
- Accepting new members: Yes (applications under review, responses expected early May 2026)
- Children allowed: No
- LGBTQ+ friendly: Yes
- Diversity explicitly valued and emphasized

### Location on Site

## Notes & Open Questions

- "Nowhere" was the historical name; "Elsewhere" is the current name (rename happened at some point — exact year unknown)
- "Norg" is legacy shorthand for "Nowhere Organization" still used by veteran attendees
- The "playa" terminology is borrowed directly from Burning Man culture
- The event emphasizes radical self-reliance but barrios like Wonderever provide communal infrastructure (shade, water, food, showers) that eases that burden for their members
- Wonderever has been a constant presence since the early days of Nowhere (2013)
- The "wonderlings" term is Wonderever-specific; the event-wide term for attendees is "nobodies"
